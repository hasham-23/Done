Find yourself in therapy

ZaraTherapy was founded in 2013 to remove the traditional barriers to therapy and make mental
health care more accessible to everyone. Today, it is the world’s largest therapy service — providing
professional, affordable, and tailored therapy in a convenient online format. ZaraTherapy network of over
30,000 therapists worldwide has helped millions of people take ownership of their mental health and
work towards their personal goals. As the unmet need for mental health services continues to grow,
BetterHelp is committed to expanding access to therapy globally.

our therapists

All of our therapists have a relevant degree in their field, at least 3 years of experience,
and have to be qualified by their respective professional organization after successfully
completing the necessary education, exams, training, and practice requirements. We refer
to therapists across our sites and apps according to their professional title and credentials,
specific to the U.S., U.K., Australia, Germany, France, or the Netherlands, as applicable.
