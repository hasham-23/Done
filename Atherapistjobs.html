About the Role

We’re seeking compassionate, licensed professionals who are passionate about supporting mental health through virtual therapy sessions.

Responsibilities

  • Conduct virtual therapy sessions via video, chat, or phone.
  • Provide personalized support based on client needs.
  • Maintain accurate records and follow-up plans.

Requirements

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s in Psychology/Counseling.
  • Valid license or certification.
  • At least 1 year of counseling experience.
  • Strong communication & empathy skills.

Perks & Benefits

  • Fully remote work with flexible hours.
  • Supportive team and wellness-focused environment.
  • Learning and development opportunities.
  • Competitive compensation per session.

Hiring Process

  1. Submit your application.
  2. Initial screening and license verification.
  3. Online interview with the onboarding team.
  4. Platform training and setup.
  5. Start helping clients!

Benefits of Joining

  • $650 monthly Health Benefit Stipend
  • Bonuses & incentives for high performers
  • Free ZARA Therapy membership
  • Free access to 390+ CEU courses
